A Laney Day: The Series




How it all started

A Laney Day was the final project for 3D Animation that was continued

into a larger series. Each animation depicts other mannerisms of Laney

in order to get practice with 2D animation and 3D world building.







Where it's going

A Laney Day: The Series will continue and be posted on Instagram (@sydney.kit.anim) regularly

and added to this page semi-frequently.

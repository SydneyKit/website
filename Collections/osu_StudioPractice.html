Studio Practice




for good

The exploration of feelings and unconventional fears is the primary driving force behind this piece. for good is a hand-drawn, two-dimensional, short film about my fear of losing my best friend in the line of duty due to the growing political instability in the United States. Throughout my works I have attempted to induce feelings in audiences rather than describing them so that the audience can connect with the emotions instead of having a superficial understanding of them. By creating this fictional film, I attempt to offer peace and resolution to myself by invoking similar emotions in the audience.

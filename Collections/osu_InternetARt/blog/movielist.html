365 Days of Movies

The Movie List Up Until March 10th

Week Eight

March 8, 2021-March 14, 2021

Welcome to the world of our madness! For our new year's resolution, since we knew that the year would start off in quarantine, we decided to challenge ourselves to watch a movie a day. To be more accurate, Tom decided he wanted to do this and I just went, "...okay!"

Tom and I love watching movies and had AMC movie passes prior to the pandemic. We would go see a movie a week during the school year and up to three movies a week during the summer. Movies have always been a big part of our relationship so it was no surprsie when we decided to do this.

I am not going to spoil the other months but here are a few hints:


As of 3/10/2021 we are 67 out of 365 days done. Today marks the 69th day (nice) so we will be watching today and yesterday's movie later to complete it.


We have not been true to watching one movie per day. School work or family life gets in the way and sometimes we've had to watch two or three movies a day to catch up. We knew this would be an issue going into it so we're not bothered by it. I'm actually surprised we haven't given up yet...!


All videos are recorded and uploaded to my personal Instagram account's story so that they don't take up all my phone's storage. It's also to share it with our friends and family who all agree that we are crazy.

I Have My Own Comic Book?!

Week Eleven

March 29, 2021-April 4, 2021






Throughout the semester I have been working on my own comic series for ART 5890 Special Topics: Webcomics course titled "Love, Me". It is a collection of advice and stories from friends and family told through various animals and touches on differenct subjects such as mental health, sexuality, deaf awareness, and more.


I had applied for a grant to help out with the cost of printing the comic book and...


I GOT IT!

Thanks to The Fergus Materials and Scholarships Fund I am able to sell the comic books at a lower price AND be able to offer discounts!



So even though this past week has been an academic break week, I have been working nonstop on my comicbook!

For class, only 10 comics are required, however to print the comic book I need 20 total pages (4 for the cover, 16 inside pages)! It has been a lot of work but will definitely pay off in the long run.



I am super excited to share my work with everyone! I will be leaving some comic books around campus April 23rd to celebrate my second dose of the COVID vaccine, so check out my instagram (@sydney.kit.anim) for your chance to get a free copy!



Thank you for reading my blog all the way through! Please use the discount code "SYDBLOG" to get $2 off your comic book order.
